# Product discovery

Goal: reduce risk on **value, usability, feasibility, viability** *before* committing to build.

## Opportunity-Solution Tree
Outcome (the metric) → Opportunities (unmet needs/pains) → Solutions → Experiments. Always tie a solution back
to the opportunity and the outcome.

## JTBD interviews
Ask about the last time they faced the problem (story, not opinions). Probe the *job*, the *struggle*, and the
*current workaround*. Avoid leading questions and feature-pitching.

## Assumption mapping
List riskiest assumptions (most-unknown × most-impactful first); design the cheapest test for each before
writing code.

## Rules
- Talk to users continuously, not once. Separate what people *say* from what they *do*.
- A solution with no validated opportunity is a guess.
