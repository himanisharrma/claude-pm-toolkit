# North-star & metric trees

- **North-star**: the single metric that best reflects *value delivered to users* (and predicts revenue).
  E.g. "weekly active teams completing a workflow", not "signups".
- **Input metrics**: 3-5 levers a team can move that drive the north-star. This is the metric *tree*.
- **Guardrails**: things that must not get worse (latency, churn, complaints, cost).

## Frameworks
- **AARRR** (Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue) — funnel lens.
- **HEART** (Happiness, Engagement, Adoption, Retention, Task-success) — UX lens.

## Rules
- Prefer metrics that resist gaming; pair every "increase X" with a guardrail.
- Distinguish leading (predictive, actionable) vs lagging (outcome) metrics.
- A metric you can't instrument yet is a TODO, not a metric — say so.
