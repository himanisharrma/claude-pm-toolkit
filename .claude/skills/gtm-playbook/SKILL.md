# GTM playbook

1. **Segment & ICP** — who buys first, why now, disqualifiers.
2. **Positioning** — for [ICP] who [need], [product] is a [category] that [key benefit], unlike [alt]
   (April Dunford style). Anchor to the competitive alternative.
3. **Pricing & packaging** — value metric, tiers, what's gated; price to value, not cost.
4. **Channels** — where the ICP already is (PLG, sales-led, partners, content).
5. **Launch sequence** — alpha → beta → GA; internal enablement, docs, support, comms, success metrics.
6. **Objection handling** — top 3 buyer objections and the response.

## Rules
- Positioning before promotion. Name the alternative you're displacing.
- Define the launch's success metric and a rollback/again plan before launch day.
